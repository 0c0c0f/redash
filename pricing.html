Is there a trial period?

Yes. All accounts come with a free 14-days trial period. No credit-card required.

What are saved queries?

Any query that you save and has a URL you can share.

What are "Pro Data Sources"?

Redshift, Vertica, Impala, Hive, Presto, Oracle and TreasureData.

We're already using Re:dash. Can we migrate our data to the hosted solution?

In case you want to migrate existing users, alerts, queries and dashboards from your Re:dash deployment, we provide you with a migration script. This script creates an export and uploads it to a secure bucket on S3, from which we recreate your data in your hosted Re:dash account.
